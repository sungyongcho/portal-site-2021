 <br \>
 <br \>
Dayoung Lee has worked in the research and planning field of convergence art program and planning with an interest in the social role of art. Lee began to work at the Art Collider Lab, Center for Art Convergence, Korea National University of Arts, from 2016 to the present, conducting research on convergence art education and overseas exchange, finally planning and operating creative support programs. She has been interested in creating a new culture at the boundary between art and technology. Since 2017, research of Playful Media based on media interactivity has been conducted and published a &ldquo;Critical Player&rdquo; series. In addition, Lee is taking a role to operate a creative support program based on a system linked to convergence art education-creation-presentation for emerging artists.
